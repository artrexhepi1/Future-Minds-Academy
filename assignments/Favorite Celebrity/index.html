About Travis Scott

Travis Scott, born Jacques Berman Webster II, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He gained fame in the late 2010s for his unconventional production techniques and his energetic stage performances.

Scott's musical style is influenced by hip hop, pop, and trap music, and he often incorporates elements of psychedelic and experimental music into his work.
